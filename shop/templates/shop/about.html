About Us

We are a fashion and clothing company based in Lahore with our hands-on services in a number of works – entailing from casual to party wear, and custom-made, our stitches speak of exemplary taste of the Asian roots – Order Indian and Pakistani style dresses from our wide range of collection. Our years of experience in the clothing and fashion industry through our brick-and-mortar stores are continuing to win the credibility of our clientele we care about. Given the ever-warming response of our clientage we have expanded our marketing – where you can purchase from anywhere in the world through our Online Store. We are happy to deliver worldwide, hassle free – experience the best shopping experience for Asian clothes with Glamline Wears.

Our Vision

Our vision strives in maintaining a promising integrity and lasting relationship with our client. We believe in building a rapport that balances well – when you trust in our products, we make sure to return the effort – and we do so by targeting superseding your expectations. At Glamline Wears, we believe in integrating conscious effort, felt in every stitch and every thread you could possibly get your hands on. This long-term relationship is the milestone that we have always aspired to achieve before, and we continue to move towards in the coming times. Our online store is a branched out effort that speaks of this expanded vision in the best of capabilities.

Future Plans
